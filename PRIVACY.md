# Privacy Policy — Tidal

_Last updated: 2026-06-20_

Tidal ("the app") is a single-player arcade game. We respect your privacy and
keep things simple.

## Data we collect
**None.** Tidal does not collect, transmit, or share any personal information.

- The app has **no user accounts** and **no login**.
- The app makes **no network requests** — it runs entirely offline.
- There are **no analytics, no advertising, and no third-party trackers**.

## Data stored on your device
Tidal saves a small amount of data **locally on your device only** (it never
leaves the device):

- Your **high score**.
- Your **settings** (sound, music, haptics, reduce motion).

This data is removed if you delete the app.

## Children's privacy
The app does not collect data from anyone, including children.

## Changes to this policy
If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted at this URL with a new
"Last updated" date.
