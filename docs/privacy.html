Privacy Policy — Tidal Orbit

Last updated: 2026-06-28

Tidal Orbit ("the app") is a single-player arcade game. We respect your privacy and keep things simple.

Data we collect

None. Tidal Orbit does not collect, transmit, or share any personal information. There are no accounts or logins, the app makes no network requests, and there is no analytics, advertising, or third-party tracking.

Data stored on your device

The app saves a small amount of data locally on your device only (it never leaves the device): your high score, your coin balance, your unlocked orbitals, and your settings (sound, music, haptics, reduce motion). This data is removed if you delete the app.

Children's privacy

The app does not collect data from anyone, including children.

Changes to this policy

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted at this URL with a new "Last updated" date.
